Career and Placement Services

Career and Placement Services (CAPS) at The Millenial School Network is an integral part of the network's program offering. Besides a strong academic backbone, the existence of a strong career placement programme ensures translating academic performances and extra-curricular achievements into local and international acceptances, need-cum-merit based scholarships and most importantly, a high university placement ratio.
TMS-CAPS contributes towards providing holistic career guidance and help students make informed decisions.
